UX Design & Branding

Moodboard
Moodboard

Whether you're planning a re-brand, packaging styles, social media posts, or any other visuals, your first step should be creating a moodboard. A moodboard is a quick visual guide that captures the desired mood and tone of your content. It can be as polished or as rough as you like! They're great for referring to when you're making design choices and they help designers create a unified look across all platforms.This moodboard is for my own website.
